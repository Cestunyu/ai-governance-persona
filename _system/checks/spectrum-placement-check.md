## Risk / Governance Questions

- Does the text recommend stopping, pausing, regulating, steering, accelerating, or simply deploying AI?
- Is the feared failure extinction, loss of control, social harm, concentration of power, stagnation, or overregulation?
- Does the text treat advanced AI as exceptional or as a normal technology?

## Humanism Questions

- Are humans the final source of value?
- Does the text emphasize democratic control, preference satisfaction, social justice, productivity, evolution, intelligence, capital, or technocultural acceleration?
- Does it treat human judgment as the thing to preserve, the thing to improve, the bottleneck to overcome, or the obstacle to route around?

## Influence Questions

- Did this text create a vocabulary other people now use?
- Did it shape institutions, labs, policy, funding, or public debate?
- Is its influence academic, institutional, cultural, capital-facing, or memetic?
- Is the influence direct or genealogical?

## Placement Output

Write one sentence:
