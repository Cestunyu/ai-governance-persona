The four spectrums inside a result

The spectrum is not a second UI anymore. It unfolds the same capability belief, risk judgment, governance position, and value position shown in the result page.

Capability Belief

Capability narrative

Whether you see current AI claims as transformative, mixed, or hype / bubble-prone.

Risk Judgment

Main risk

Whether you center civilizational risk, current social harm, ordinary technology failure, or the opportunity cost of restriction.

Governance Position

Response style

Whether you prefer pause and strong control, audits and regulation, contextual governance, or more open deployment and decentralized building.

Value Position

Value center

Where you place human control, social accountability, technical learning dynamics, intelligence expansion, or posthuman transition.
