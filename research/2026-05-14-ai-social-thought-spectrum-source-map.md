# AI Social Thought Spectrum Source Map

Search date: 2026-05-14

This source map seeds a learning/reading project on AI social thought: x-risk safety, critical AI, Bitter Lesson scaling thought, pragmatic optimism, techno-optimism, e/acc, and anti-humanist accelerationism.

Main project repository: `ai-ideology-quiz`

## Collection Principle

Collect primary texts first. Add secondary commentary only when it helps locate influence, genealogy, or institutional uptake.

For every candidate text, record:

- thinker or movement
- primary claim
- risk model
- theory of human agency
- policy or institutional recommendation
- spectrum coordinates
- influence channel
- strongest counterposition

## Influence Assessment Method

Do not rely on vibe. Score influence by channel:

- academic citations
- field formation
- institutional adoption
- government or policy uptake
- frontier-lab practice
- capital/startup influence
- public discourse
- movement formation
- memetic influence

Use 5 only when a thinker or text plausibly shaped the field's categories, institutions, or public debate. Use 3 for memetic or genealogical sources that matter but do not directly organize mainstream AI work.
